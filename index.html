Common Questions

Is it safe for solo travelers?

Absolutely. We specialize in safe, local-led experiences for solo explorers.

What should I wear?

Comfortable walking shoes, light clothing, and sun protection are recommended.

Do you offer private tours?

Yes, we offer custom private tours for families, couples, and groups.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance, especially during peak tourist season.

What languages do you offer tours in?

Tours are primarily conducted in English, but we can arrange tours in French, Spanish, and local languages upon request.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before the tour. Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a 50% fee.
